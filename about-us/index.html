Meet Telgorithm.

Established in 2021, Telgorithm was created by telecom industry veterans who, with 20+ years of collective experience, saw a massive opportunity to set a new standard for quality and transparency in the space.

Our mission is simple.

We're making enterprise-grade technology accessible to the masses. Our messaging APIs are easier to use and more affordable than ever so you can focus on running your business.

We’ve been the customer before.

We're all too familiar of the red tape, ambiguities, unanticipated fees, and rigid contracts. We couldn't find the solutions we were looking for, so we created Telgorithm.
