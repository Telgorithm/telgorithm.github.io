Partnered with The Campaign Registry

Partnered with The Campaign Registry to ensure compliant, secure, and effective messaging campaigns, enhancing trust and reliability for your brand.
Unlike other Connectivity Partners (CNPs), Telgorithm is connected to TCR on the backend to enable Automated 10DLC Registration.
When you register with TCR and select Telgorithm as your CNP, your Brand and Campaign info is automatically sent to Telgorithm.
This means you only have to enter that info once, saving your team time and reducing the risk of errors.
