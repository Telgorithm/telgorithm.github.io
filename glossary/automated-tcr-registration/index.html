Automated 10DLC Registration

Definition

As early 10DLC adopters, we knew how important it was to make The Campaign Registry (TCR) registration as quick and painless as possible, so we partnered with TCR for an API-based registration called Automated 10DLC Registration that saves our customers time, money, and unnecessary overhead.

How this works is unlike other Connectivity Partners (CNPs), Telgorithm is connected to TCR on the backend. When you register with TCR as a Campaign Service Provider (CSP) and select Telgorithm as your CNP, your Brand and Campaign info is automatically sent to Telgorithm. This means you only have to enter the information once, saving your team time and reducing the risk of errors.
