What makes Telgorithm different?

Like Twilio, Telgorithm offers messaging APIs to enable 10DLC texting for your software or application users. The difference is that Telgorithm specializes in A2P 10DLC messaging and we provide professional services including compliance guidance and hands-on support to all of our customers. 

So if you haven’t been happy with Twilio 10DLC – explore how Telgorithm is the best alternative to handle your 10DLC messaging.

10DLC Deliverability TechnologyOur patent-pending Smart Queueing technology prevents your customers from exceeding their 10DLC Carrier limits and saves an average of 20% of 10DLC messages from failing, improving your customer’s deliverability and per-message savings.Twilio customers often experience deliverability issues with no explanation or transparency. The Twilio 10DLC error code 30023 occurs because your customer has exceeded their Carrier rate limits, resulting in dropped messages.
Easy to SwitchEasily port active or existing phone numbers from another API provider to Telgorithm instantly and in bulk with Automated Number Porting, completely free.

Our Porting API is connected at the Carrier level in real time, which allows our customers to code webhooks into their software so they know when the second a number is ported to avoid disruptions.		Twilio doesn't allow for instant porting and requires 24-48 hour holds on all port requests due to the nature of a manual process. This is because Twilio does not offer a Porting API that is connected at the Carrier level in real time. Due to this setup, Twilio customers must alert their provider manually. This prevents your application from automatically being alerted and causing porting errors.

How long does it take to integrate with Telgorithm’s SMS APIs?

Our SMS APIs are developer-friendly and easy to integrate with. Integration is a 3-step process:


  1. You must register or be registered as a Campaign Service Provider (CSP) with The Campaign Registry (TCR).
  2. Once Telgorithm is selected as your Connectivity Partner (CNP) in TCR, you can integrate with Telgorithm with minimal coding. Telgorithm will automatically import your approved Campaigns into your Telgorithm account. You can now purchase numbers.
  3. Test inbound and outbound text messages, get any questions answered by our technical or support teams, and experience the Telgorithm magic!
